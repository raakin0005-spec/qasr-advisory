How does a university admissions consultant help a student in Qatar?

Qasr builds one coherent plan across university selection, profile development, application systems, written materials, deadlines, interviews and final decisions. The advice reflects the student’s curriculum, academic record, ambitions and family priorities.

College counseling for Doha and Qatar families

International-school students in Qatar may follow IB, A level, AP, American or other curricula while considering several countries at once. That creates choices about subjects, testing, activities, cost and application timing. Qasr helps families understand what matters for each destination and focus effort where it can have the greatest value.

Questions Qatar families ask

Do you meet students in Doha?

Qasr’s private consulting is delivered online, so students and parents across Doha and Qatar can meet regularly without travel.

Do you support IB and A level students?

Yes. Strategy is adapted for IB, A level, AP, American diploma and other curricula, along with each student’s target countries.

Can you help us compare the US and UK?

Yes. We compare degree structure, application style, flexibility, cost and the student’s academic preferences before recommending a direction.

When should a Qatar student start?

Early planning in Grades 9 to 11 provides the most room for purposeful profile development. Active Grade 12 applicants can receive application-cycle support.
