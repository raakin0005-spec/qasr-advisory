Questions families ask first.

These answers should later become a full FAQ hub for SEO and parent education.

Do you only work with students applying to Ivy League or Oxbridge?

No. We support students targeting highly selective universities, but we also help families find excellent-fit universities where the student can thrive academically, socially, and professionally.

Do you support destinations outside the US and UK?

Yes. Our primary focus is the US, UK, and Europe, but we can also advise families considering Malaysia, Turkey, Netherlands, China, Qatar, UAE, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and other destinations.

Do you support both undergraduate and master’s applications?

Yes. Qasr supports undergraduate and master’s applicants across multiple global destinations.

Do you work with families in Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and the UAE?

Yes. The advisory model is built with GCC families in mind, including parent involvement, international school curricula, WhatsApp communication, and country-specific planning.

Do you write essays for students?

No. We help students develop, structure, and refine their work while preserving their own voice and meeting ethical admissions standards.
