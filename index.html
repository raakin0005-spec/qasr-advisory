Questions families ask first.

Direct answers about Qasr’s university admissions consulting, application systems, destinations, timing and fees.

What does a university admissions consultant help with?

Qasr helps students build an application strategy, choose suitable universities, plan timelines, develop their academic and extracurricular profile, prepare application materials and make a confident final university decision.

When should a student begin university admissions planning?

Students benefit most from beginning 12 to 24 months before their intended university start date, although focused support is also available for applicants already in an active admissions cycle.

Does Qasr support Common App and UCAS applications?

Yes. Qasr advises students applying through the Common App for US universities and UCAS for UK universities, including university selection, application positioning, written materials, activities, references and interview preparation.

Does Qasr work with students in Saudi Arabia, Qatar and the UAE?

Yes. Qasr works with students and families across Saudi Arabia, Qatar, the UAE and the wider GCC through private online consultations and structured application support.

Which study destinations does Qasr cover?

Qasr advises on universities in the United States, United Kingdom, Europe, Canada, Australia, Singapore, Malaysia, Turkey, China, Qatar and other international destinations.

Does Qasr support both undergraduate and master’s applicants?

Yes. Support is available for undergraduate, transfer and master’s applicants, with the strategy adapted to the degree level, destination and application system.

How much does university admissions consulting cost?

Pricing depends on the applicant’s timeline, number of universities and level of support. Qasr recommends the appropriate package and confirms a quote after a complimentary consultation.

How do I begin working with Qasr Advisory?

Submit the consultation form or contact Qasr on WhatsApp. The first conversation reviews the student’s background, goals, target countries and timeline before a support plan is recommended.
