Frequently asked questions.

These should eventually become a full searchable FAQ hub with 80–100 questions for students and parents.

Do you support both undergraduate and master’s admissions?

Yes. Qasr supports students applying to undergraduate and master’s programs across the US, UK, and Europe.

Do you work with students in Saudi Arabia and Qatar?

Yes. The advisory model is designed especially for families in Saudi Arabia, Qatar, UAE, and the wider GCC, while also welcoming students globally.

Can parents join meetings?

Yes. Parent involvement is encouraged, especially for country selection, investment decisions, deadlines, and final enrollment choices.

Do you write essays for students?

No. We help students develop, structure, and refine their essays while preserving their authentic voice and meeting ethical admissions standards.

Can you help with scholarships?

Yes. We can help identify scholarship opportunities, refine scholarship essays, and position applications strategically.

How early should we start?

For undergraduate admissions, one to three years before application season is ideal. For master’s admissions, six to twelve months is a strong starting point.

Do you help with Oxbridge or Ivy League applications?

Yes. We support students targeting highly selective universities, including Ivy League, Oxbridge, and other leading institutions, while building realistic balanced lists.

Is the first consultation free?

Yes. The first consultation is complimentary and helps determine whether Qasr is the right fit for the student’s goals.
