What does undergraduate admissions support include?

Qasr helps students organise university selection, application strategy, timelines, activities, written materials, school documents, interviews and final decisions into one coherent process.

Support across the full application journey

Strong applications begin with fit. We consider academic interests, curriculum, predicted or achieved grades, family budget, preferred destinations, campus environment and longer-term goals before shaping the university list.

University and course selection

Build a balanced shortlist based on genuine academic and personal fit, admission expectations, affordability and destination preferences.

Common App and US college applications

Coordinate the application profile, activities, main written response, university supplements, recommendations, testing requirements and deadlines.

UCAS and UK university applications

Clarify course choice, compare entry requirements, plan the UCAS application, develop the academic narrative and prepare for interviews where required.

Europe and other global destinations

Map direct application requirements, English-taught programs, document needs and deadlines across institutions and countries.

Interviews and final decisions

Prepare for admissions conversations and compare offers using academic fit, total cost, student experience and future opportunity.
