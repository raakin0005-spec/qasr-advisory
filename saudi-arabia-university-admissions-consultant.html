What does a university admissions consultant in Saudi Arabia do?

A university admissions consultant turns a complicated international process into a clear plan. Qasr connects the student’s curriculum, grades, interests, activities, family priorities and budget to a balanced university list and a structured application strategy.

College counseling for Saudi students applying abroad

Students in Saudi Arabia often compare several systems at once: the Common App for US colleges, UCAS for UK universities and direct applications across Europe and other destinations. Each system evaluates students differently. Our role is to help families understand those differences early and make decisions in the right order.

We work privately online with students in Riyadh, Jeddah and across the Kingdom, including students following IB, A level, American diploma, AP and national curricula. Guidance is adapted to the student rather than based on a generic university list.

US university admissions from Saudi Arabia

For US applications, we help students evaluate academic fit, admissions selectivity, affordability and campus preferences; organise Common App components; position activities and achievements; plan recommendations and testing; develop written materials; and coordinate university-specific requirements.

Explore our Common App consulting for GCC students and the US admissions roadmap for GCC families.

UK university and UCAS guidance

UK applications begin with the course. We help students compare subjects and entry requirements, create a balanced UCAS selection, organise the application’s academic evidence and prepare for interviews where required. The strategy is especially important when a student is deciding between specialised UK degrees and the broader flexibility of the US system.

See our UCAS consulting service.

Profile building in Grades 9, 10 and 11

Early profile planning is not about collecting random certificates. It is about building academic direction and sustained involvement. We help students decide where to deepen an interest through reading, research, competitions, projects, service, leadership, internships or summer experiences—while protecting academic performance and personal wellbeing.

A balanced university list, not a prestige list

A strong list includes ambitious, realistic and more secure choices that the student would genuinely be happy to attend. We compare admission expectations alongside course quality, total cost, scholarships, location, student life and future goals. No ethical consultant can guarantee an admission decision, but a disciplined process can help a student present their strongest authentic application.

Questions Saudi families ask

Do you offer college counseling in Riyadh?

Yes. Qasr works online with students and parents in Riyadh and across Saudi Arabia, allowing regular private meetings without requiring travel.

Can Saudi students use the Common App?

Yes. Many US colleges accept international applications through the Common App. Students submit core information and then complete each university’s additional requirements.

How early should we hire an admissions consultant?

Grades 9 to 11 offer the greatest room for profile development and careful course planning. Students in Grade 12 can still receive focused support for an active application cycle.

How much does university admissions consulting cost in Saudi Arabia?

Fees depend on the student’s stage, target systems, number of universities and level of support. Qasr confirms scope and pricing after a complimentary consultation.
